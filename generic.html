Hey! I’m Dhruv Agarwal, a passionate Computer Science and Engineering undergrad currently studying at SKIT Jaipur. From an early age, technology has always sparked my curiosity, and now I’m pursuing my dream to explore the depths of computer science. Whether it’s building software or solving complex problems, I’m all in.
What drives me is the sheer possibility of what can be built with code. I’m someone who picks up new concepts quickly when I’m genuinely curious, and while I might not always broadcast my achievements, I’m constantly striving to reach my full potential.
I believe in working hard and smart, but sometimes, like many others, I struggle with motivation. Still, I’m focused on pushing past those moments and getting better each day. Outside of my academic life, I’m always seeking ways to improve myself, whether it’s through fitness, exploring new tech, or figuring out how to stay consistent in everything I do.
When I’m not coding, you’ll find me lifting weights, learning about new tech trends, or working on personal projects that challenge my creativity. My goal? To grow, not just as an engineer, but as a person who contributes meaningful solutions to the world.
